OASIS IN THE CITY OF NIZAMS

Nestled in the elite enclave of Banjara Hills, is Taj Deccan, Hyderabad, one of the finest luxury hotels in Hyderabad. Spread over six acres of manicured gardens, with the perfect blend of sophistication and elegance, it offers a haven of tranquility in the heart of the city. Close to landmark attractions, it is 30 km away from the airport and short drive away from Secunderabad, making it ideal for both business and leisure travellers. Enter a one of its kind three-winged atrium lobby where contemporary touches intermingle with local motifs, beautiful artwork and gorgeous chandeliers, highlighting understated luxury. Our 156 spacious rooms and suites too, are exquisitely designed with modern comforts and plush furnishings to ensure a relaxing retreat. The Taj Deccan restaurants will take you on a culinary journey, with our skilled chefs crafting delectable dishes from Asian to continental and regional to coastal delicacies, while the Atrium Bar ensures your favourite tipple is never too far. The perfect mix of venue options with our inimitable service ensures we set the standard for high octane as well as intimate celebrations and meetings. When you are not working, enjoy the spa and fitness centre or try a game of a tennis and relax by the pool for ultimate indulgence. Taj Deccan, Hyderabad, with its many amenities and thoughtful touches makes for an exceptional stay in the City of Nawabs.
